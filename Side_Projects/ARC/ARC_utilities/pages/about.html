About Game Jam

Urbana Game Jam is a virtual game jam for high school students. It will take place between May 21-28. A game jam is an event where participants collaborate to create games. Your creative work will be guided by a central theme revealed before the game jam starts. A game jam focuses on rapid game development, allowing participants to quickly discover new talents, enjoy programming and artistry, and challenge themselves.

To sign up, fill out the registration form with your personal email account and join the Discord. Email urbanagamejam@gmail.com with any questions.

Purpose

Our team noticed a niche in computer science classes that was not explored more: the learning ability of games. We are interested in promoting the fun and engaging aspects of computer science and tapping into the creative side of every participant. A game jam is an easy and fun way to meet like-minded individuals and learn at your own pace on your own time.
